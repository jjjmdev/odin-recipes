How to cook KALDERETA

Beef Caldereta / Kaldereta is one of the most craved Filipino beef stew recipes in almost every home. It's a hearty, filling Filipino food made with beef as the main ingredient, carrots, liver spread, and bell peppers stewed in a tomato-based sauce.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs beef shanks (2 pieces of beef shank)
  • 2 lbs of ox tail
  • 2-inch ginger
  • 1 medium onion
  • 8 garlic cloves
  • 1-2 large carrots
  • 1-2 bell peppers
  • 1 can tomato sauce (398 mL)
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 cup frozen peas
  • ½ cup of red wine (optional, highly recommended)
  • ¼ cup fish sauce
  • 1 tablespoon liver spread (or liver paste)
  • 1 jalapeno (optional, highly recommended)
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper

Steps

  1. In a large Dutch oven, brown your beef pieces in batches. Do not overcrowd.
  2. Remove and set aside. Discard the excess oil.
  3. Add all the ingredients except carrots, bell peppers, and green peas.
  4. Let it come to a boil. Reduce the temperature to a low simmer. Cover with lid.
  5. Let it simmer for 1 hour, and check to ensure nothing is sticking to the bottom from
    6. time to time.
  6. When the beef is fork tender, skim some of the oil from the top.
  7. Add carrots and bell peppers. Let it cook for 15 minutes.
  8. In the last 2 minutes, add in your green peas. Adjust seasoning and salt and pepper
    9. accordingly.
  9. Serve this with white rice.
